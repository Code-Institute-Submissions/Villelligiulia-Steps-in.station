How many good reasons do you need to start doing something? Here we are going to reveal you 10 good reasons why going for a walk is so good for your well being both mental and physical. Even 10 minutes a day can have an impact on your health. The benefits of regular walking are huge. It doesn't matter how far you can get or how much time you can dedicate to it, as long as it turns into an amazing habit...

Walking can help you burn calories. Burning calories can help you maintain or lose weight.

Regular walking can help to reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as cardiovascular disease, type 2 diabetes and some cancers.

Walking increases oxygen flow through the body. It can also increase levels of cortisol, epinephrine, and norepinephrine. Those are the hormones that help elevate energy levels.

In addition to all of these benefits, walking more can have a positive environmental impact, and itâ€™s free!

Walking can strengthen the muscles in your legs. To build up more strength, walk in a hilly area or on a treadmill with an incline. Or find routes with stairs.

Walking is a weight bearing exercise and improves and maintains bone density, it can help reduce the risk of osteoporosis and fractures.

Regular movement throughout the day can improve sleep quality. Exercise and exposure to natural light keeps our sleep, wake patterns in order and helps us to feel tired at night.

Exercise releases a chemical called endorphins, these stimulate relaxation and can help to reduce stress and improve your mood.

Walking outside gives us the added benefit of nature, research has shown that people who spend more time in nature are likely to feel happier

Walking may help clear your head and help you think creatively.
